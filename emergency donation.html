EMERGENCY BLOOD DONATION

What greater gift is there than the gift of life? That’s exactly what every blood donation delivers. Blood and blood products are a critical part of everyday medical care including major surgeries, medical procedures, cancer treatments and managing disease. For patients like Hunter, the need is constant. Born with a congenital heart disease, Madison needed more than 40 blood transfusions as a baby, as well as daily platelet and plasma transfusions.
